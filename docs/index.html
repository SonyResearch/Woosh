Woosh - A Sound Effect Foundation Model

Gaetan Hadjeres1, Marc Ferras1, Khaled Koutini1, Benno Weck-Hufnagel1, Alexandre Bittar1, Thomas Hummel1, Zineb Lahrichi1, Hakim Missoum1, Joan Serrà1, Yuki mitsufuji1,2,
1Sony AI
2Sony Group Corporation
Code arXiv

Abstract

The audio research community relies on open generative models to build upon and use as baselines for novel approaches. In this report, we describe the publicly released audio generative models built at Sony AI, including architecture, training process and evaluation against other popular models. Being optimized for sound effect generation, we provide a high-quality audio encoder/decoder model, a text-audio alignment model for conditioning, and text-to-audio (T2A) and video-to-audio (V2A) generative models. Distilled T2A and V2A models for fast inference are also included in the release. Our evaluation shows competitive performance against Stable Audio Open and Tango-Flux models. Inference code and open weights are available on https://www.github.com/woosh.
